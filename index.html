Mobility solutions

Battery-operated electric tow tugs are increasingly popular across industries for their efficiency, eco-friendliness, and versatility.

Eco-Friendly Zero Emissions

Being battery-powered, electric tow tugs produce no exhaust emissions, making them ideal for indoor use and contributing to a cleaner work environment.

Reduced Carbon Footprint

They contribute to lower overall emissions, aligning with sustainability goals in many industries.

Heavy Duty Load Capacity

Tow tugs can handle a wide range of weights, from a few hundred kilograms to multiple tons, making them ideal for transporting various types of goods.

Heavy Equipment Movement

Tow tugs are used to move heavy artillery, radar systems, and other military equipment across bases and training grounds.

Aircraft Towing

In military airfields, tow tugs are used for towing fighter jets, transport planes, and helicopters. They ensure smooth and safe movement of aircraft on the ground, minimizing risks of damage.

Adaptability to Rough Terrain

Military-grade tow tugs are often designed with rugged tires, reinforced frames, and high torque drives to maneuver through challenging terrains, which is crucial for defense operations.

Solutions offered

We have offered customized solutions to various Customers over the years to eliminate the hazard of falls at workplace

Roof Hatch Systems

Our roof hatch systems are crafted for seamless access and safety. Designed to meet or exceed OSHA and industry standards, our hatches are durable, weather-resistant, and come with built-in safety features, ensuring the safest point of entry for roof access.

Skylight Protection

Skylights are beautiful and functional but can also present significant risks. Our skylight protection solutions are engineered to prevent accidental falls and meet industry safety regulations, offering maximum visibility while safeguarding your team.

Railing Systems

Our railing systems are customized to suit a variety of structures, from rooftops to walkways. Built to withstand harsh conditions, they provide reliable protection against falls, with aesthetic options to seamlessly integrate with your existing architecture.

Horizontal Lifeline Systems

Designed for workers operating in elevated environments, our horizontal lifeline systems provide the freedom to move along a designated path while maintaining secure fall protection. These systems are fully adaptable to different environments, offering ease of use and exceptional safety.

Skyhook Anchor Points

For unique roof access and positioning needs, our Skyhook anchor points deliver a flexible and robust solution. Engineered for reliable anchorage, they are compatible with various lifeline systems and keep workers secure in hard-to-reach areas.

Walkway Solutions

These are designed for modern roof types including trapezoidal profile composite or built up and standing seam metal roofs. It can be used on flat, barrel, or sloping roofs, with steps and a traverse option so you can create access to virtually any roof configuration from 0 to 35 degrees.

Not only does it protect those accessing the roof, but it also protects the roof sheet itself. It does this thanks to its clearly defined access path eliminating foot traffic on the roof sheet.

Monkey Ladder Vertical Lifeline Systems

Our monkey ladder lifeline systems are built to provide consistent protection for workers climbing vertical structures. Easy to install and designed to support safe vertical mobility, these systems ensure a safer ascent and descent, minimizing fall risk.

Overhead Lifeline Solutions

Ideal for environments with limited space and high mobility needs, our overhead lifeline systems support secure access for maintenance and operations. Designed for high endurance, these systems ensure workers’ safety from above, offering continuous protection.

Railing Protections

When working at height, our railing protections provide unparalleled safety. Crafted to comply with safety regulations, these railings protect against falls while allowing for unobstructed work along roofs and elevated walkways.
